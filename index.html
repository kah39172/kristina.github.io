About Me

My name is Kristina and I am a sophomore at UGA, majoring in Sport Management. My main passion is horseback riding. I started riding when I was 4 years old and it has been my life ever since. I used to travel all around the southeast competing at shows, in hopes of one day making the NCAA UGA Equestrian team. As a freshman, I tried out for the team and to my surprise I made it. I had worked my whole life to accomplish this goal, and I finally saw the hardwork pay off. Being on the team was one of the best experiences I could've asked for, and has made my college experience unforgettable.
